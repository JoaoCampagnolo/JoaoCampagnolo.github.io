Background

Scientific training, computational bias.

I completed a PhD in Neuroscience at the University of Copenhagen after training in Biomedical Engineering and Biophysics. My work has involved experimental design, behavioral analysis, whole-brain imaging, machine learning, multivariate statistics, and scientific programming in Python.

I am especially interested in projects that combine rigorous methods with useful computational tools — whether in neuroscience, biomedical data, or independent side projects that start as curiosity and become unexpectedly serious.
