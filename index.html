Background

Scientific training, computational bias.

I completed a PhD in Neuroscience at the University of Copenhagen, after training in Biomedical Engineering and Biophysics. My work has involved experimental design, image-based neuroscience, behavior quantification, machine learning, multivariate statistics, and scientific programming in Python.

I am particularly interested in building projects that sit between rigorous analysis and good design: things that work well, explain themselves clearly, and do not visually scream for help.
